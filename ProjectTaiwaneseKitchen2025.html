Taiwanese Kitchen

What do you do in Taiwanese Kitchen VR?

This virtual reality (VR) experience is designed for learners of Mandarin Chinese. The goal is for learners to acquire the procedural knowledge of cooking scrambled eggs and tomatoes in an authentic Taiwanese kitchen, which hinges on learners' understanding of cooking instructions in Mandarin Chinese, especially their grasp of the ba construction or the disposal construction in Chinese.

Taiwanese Kitcehn Demo Video

Background and Rationale

I had the wonderful opportunity to help create this learning experience. Similar to Japanese Office, it utilizes high-immersion VR to fully engage the user in a virtual environment, nearly completely shutting out the outside world. This highly immersive and embodied experience is expected to result in meaningful learning. More specifically, it should be conducive to building and integrating long-term context-sensitive and sensorimotor-rich memories, which in turn should facilitate automaticity and cultural appropriateness in future real-time language use.
