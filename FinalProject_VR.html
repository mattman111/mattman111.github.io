Taiwanese Kitchen VR

What do you do in Taiwanese Kitchen VR?

This virtual reality (VR) experience is designed for learners of Mandarin Chinese. The goal is for learners to acquire the procedural knowledge of cooking scrambled eggs and tomatoes in an authentic Taiwanese kitchen, which hinges on learners' understanding of cooking instructions in Mandarin Chinese, especially their grasp of the ba construction or the disposal construction in Chinese.

Demo Video

Background and Rationale

Here at Shanshan Chinese, we believe that what we do in a language and how we do them are embedded in a social communicative context. Therefore, our Chinese lessons emphasize context building and behavioral performance. Compared to using PowerPoint slides and verbal descriptions to present context and elicit performance, VR’s ability to create an immediate sense of “presence” allows the learner to just “be there,” embodying the communicative role and interpreting the meaning of an utterance or behavior by directly accessing the relevant contextual information.

Due to the firm demands for contextualization and embodiment in real-time language use, the current project uses high immersion VR—where the user wears a VR headset and is immersed in the virtual environment created by the headset while the outside world is almost completely shut out—to create context and highlight important aspects of context (e.g., roles of the interlocutors, time and place, physical objects, etc.) to which learners’ attention should be drawn.

It is expected that such highly immersive and embodied experience will result in meaningful learning. More specifically, such learning experience should be conducive to building and integrating long-term context-sensitive and sensorimotor-rich memories in the learner’s mind, which in turn should be conducive to achieving automaticity and cultural appropriateness when the learner engages in real-time language use in the future.

Learning Cycle

Three pillars are involved to help you successfully learn to use Chinese to cook a homestyle dish with a Taiwanese friend:

  • Anki flashcards: Learn the relevant vocabulary words using one of the most powerful spaced-repetition software.
  • Taiwanese kitchen VR: Immerse in an authentic kitchen environment where you interact with 3D objects representative of Taiwanese culture and cook scrambled eggs and tomatoes by following the cooking instructions from your avatar friend.
  • One-on-one tutoring session with Shanshan: Consolidate what you've learned in VR through doing application activities with Shanshan.
